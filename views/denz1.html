DENZ 1
With his roots in the graffiti and design disciplines, DENZ 1 is an accomplished hip-hop producer. DENZ 1 also handles Stay Savage behind the scenes with Samuel Gatara.
Work
His first release “Hydrate” [2016] gained serious attention on YouTube and Soundcloud. The record features fellow Stay Savage member KVKA and critically acclaimed NZ hip act Name UL. DENZ 1 toured NZ as KVKA's DJ and produced a joint project titled “Outliers” which released in 2016.
